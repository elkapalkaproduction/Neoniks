“It's a tracking device,” she explained. “I have a map of Bottomless Island and we'll be able to see where you are on it. There is an intercom system on the Sky Scooter, so if you get lost, you can always ask me for directions.”

Zoe opened another drawer in her shell and pulled out a large red button. “Here, take this!” she told Jay.

He pressed the button and a glowing 3-D image of Bottomless Island appeared in front of them.

“Cool!” exclaimed Phoebe, impressed.

She gave Zoe her last bit of magic in payment and mounted the Sky Scooter. As soon as Phoebe carefully pulled the handlebars, the scooter flew up, humming quietly.

“Hey, it works!” said Jay, showing his sister a glowing dot on the map. “I can see you!”

“Then I'll be on my way,” replied Phoebe. “Don't let me out of your sight!”

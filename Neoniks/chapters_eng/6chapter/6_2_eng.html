“I've seen the posters, of course,” said Harold thoughtfully. “Once upon a time Justacreep was my favorite actor and I even made a bet with my friends that he'd win the Golden Blob Award for acting. But he didn't get any awards at all.”

“Why not?” wondered Phoebe. “He is very talented.”

“Two years ago he put a spell on an actress. She was performing a very tricky number and she almost crashed. So Justacreep was stripped of his magic powers and now no theater wants anything to do with him.”

Phoebe and Jay looked at each other.

“Do you know where we can find him?” asked Jay.

“I heard that he now works in a locker room at the Ice Stadium,” said Harold. “He sharpens claws for the fire hockey players. Maybe you should try there?”

“The Ice Stadium is too far for us to fly to,” said Phoebe, upset. “Our wings are not strong enough and it will take too long.”

“I can carry you there,” offered Harold. “I can run really fast.”

“Thank you!” exclaimed Phoebe and Jay and climbed on his back.

“Hold tight!” said the dragon and took off down the street at full speed.

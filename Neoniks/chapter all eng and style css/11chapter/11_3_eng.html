“Don't forget the gloves or your hands will give you away,” said Wanda.

“Like I wouldn't know that,” grumbled Justacreep as he pulled the gloves on. Now he looked as if he were a real old leoman, just overdressed a little, his cloak covering his entire body.

All this time Mystie had been motionless around Wanda's neck. Her paws were tied and she could not get away.

Suddenly, they heard hurried steps and her heart quickened: Could help be on its way? Maybe someone would come and set her free?

A young Star Fairy appeared among the trees. She held a staff with a knob that looked like a saucer. It had an old man's face on one side and a young man's face on the other.

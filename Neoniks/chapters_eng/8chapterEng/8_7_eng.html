Jay’s worried voice came over the intercom, “What happened?”

“A-a-ah!” screamed Phoebe. “I’ve been attacked!”

The soldiers kept throwing their spears at the Sky Scooter and by now it looked like a bur with little thorns.

“I see where you are now!” cried Jay. “I’ll tell you where to go. Turn right… now duck… Slow down and go down!”

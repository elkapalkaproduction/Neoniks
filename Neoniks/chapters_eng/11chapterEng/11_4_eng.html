Lola the Star Fairies’ apprentice
A young Star Fairy appeared among the trees. She held a staff with a knob that looked like a saucer. It had an old man’s face on one side and a young man’s face on the other.

“Stop! Who’s there?” cried the girl.

Mystie wanted to yell to attract attention, but Wanda squeezed her jaws with her fingers.

“So you’re still alive?” she hissed. “Then be quiet or I’ll make a bath rug out of you!”

When the girl saw that it was just a leoman, she calmed down right away. “Hello! Did something happen? I saw a bright flare above.”

It looked like she hadn’t realized what happened to the starry flower. Her magic staff was just fine – the paralyzing energy had not reached the plantation.

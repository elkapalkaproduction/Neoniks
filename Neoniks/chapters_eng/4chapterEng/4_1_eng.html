CHAPTER 4

THE DOUGHNUT CAFÉ

The next morning, Jay and Phoebe went to school, but no one was there. “Summer Break,” read a note on the teachers’ lounge.

“I know who we can ask about Mr. Justacreep,” said Phoebe. “Teachers often have coffee at the Doughnut Café. Maybe someone saw him there.”

They flew across the street and went into a building that had a huge tray of doughnuts towering above it.

Both kids and adults really liked to go there because there was no other place where you could find such delicious Sweet Dreams, Hot Topics, and Sparkling Water-Under-the-Bridge. These were the Neoniks’ favorite treats.

“Where do I go now?” asked Phoebe.

“Forward!” replied Jay.

Phoebe pulled the handlebars, Sky Scooter jerked, and she hit a wet sheet that was hanging out on the clothesline.

Phoebe fell off the Sky Scooter, and the last thing that Jay heard was her yell of “Mo-o-mmy!” that was getting fainter and fainter.

“She'll crash!” gasped Jay, looking at the glowing dot on the map that was falling into the chasm.

“She'll be fine,” said Zoe confidently. “The thick green air won't let her crash – everything moves too slowly down there.”

A very out-of-breath Harold ran up to them. “I'm done with all the playbills and thought I'd come back to help,” he said. “I was worried sick about you.”

“Phoebe fell off the Sky Scooter!” cried Jay. “Please come with me to Bottomless Island! We have to save her!”

Luckily, Harold had a bit of magic with him and it was just enough for a self-propelled inflatable banana.

“You look for Phoebe and I'll call for help,” said Zoe. “You can take my map with you.”

Harold and Jay climbed aboard the banana and went off towards Bottomless Island.

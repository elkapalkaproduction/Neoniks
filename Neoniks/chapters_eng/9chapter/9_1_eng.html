CHAPTER 9

THE DEMONITE SUBMARINE

After teleporting to Bottomless Island, Justacreep spread out Furcoat like a parachute and jumped into the green abyss.

They floated down for a long time, until they reached a large stone ledge that looked like a dinner plate stuck in a wall. Wild crocorhines lived there – surly animals with crocodile jaws and rhinoceros bodies. They had sharp horns on their muzzles and their sides were covered in heavy plates that glowed with bright amber.

Crocorhines lived in a maze of caves, which few people were brave enough to enter. But Furcoat knew how to deal with these animals: they adored lacegrass.

The crocorhines had become used to the treat and, seeing Justacreep's top hat, ran up to meet their friend.

“Ho, you brutes, how are you doing?” he asked as he fed them lacegrass leaves. The crocorhines slurped and grunted with pleasure.

After giving out treats, Justacreep slunk to the cave entrance. It was completely dark and Furcoat ordered Wanda to get the magic light going.

“I don't wanna,” she whined. “I look so stupid in the magic light.”

“Are we supposed to wander around in the dark just to please you?” grumbled Justacreep.

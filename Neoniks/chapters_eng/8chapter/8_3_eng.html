The turtle waved to her new clients. “Hi, my name is Zoe Hurryfoot. We specialize in instant shipping and transportation.”

“We need to get to Bottomless Island as soon as possible,” said Jay.

Zoe looked at him doubtfully. “How old are you?”

“Jay's ten and I am twelve,” replied Phoebe.

Zoe pulled out one of her shell plates as if it were a desk drawer. “Here, read this,” she said, taking out a piece of paper entitled The Guidelines.

It said that children under twelve could only use magic transportation when accompanied by an adult.

“You, boy, will have to stay ashore, and you, girl, can take the Sky Scooter,” said Zoe and pointed to a strange vehicle that looked like an overgrown snail. “It's made of pure magic.”

Phoebe looked at it hesitantly. “I can't even imagine driving that!”

“Don't worry!” Zoe waved her away. “Driving the Sky Scooter is child's play.”

Jay grabbed Phoebe's hand. “Please go to Bottomless Island and find my fox!”

“What if I get lost?” said Phoebe doubtfully. She did not expect to have to go there on her own.

Zoe gave her an amulet with a piglet's snout and little wings. “Oink-oink!” it grunted.

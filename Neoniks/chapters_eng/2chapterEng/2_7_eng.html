“Well, are you scared?” shrieked Wanda in a dreadful voice.

“No,” said the kids.

They had all known the story of the Greedy Miner since their kindergarten days. Moreover, many of them had met him: so there was a skeleton who wandered the streets and moaned. So what?

“I don’t think Wanda’s a real teacher,” whispered Jay to Beppo. “She doesn’t know how to deal with kids.”

“Yeah, and Mr. Justacreep is pretty strange, too,” agreed Beppo. “I wonder where he’s gone to.”

Although Jay decided not to show Mystie to anyone, he was so bored with Wanda’s “lesson” that he could not take it anymore.

“Beppo, I created a magic fox,” he said quietly.

“No way!”

Beppo knew about his friend’s dream, but he thought that Jay was simply wasting magic and Will Power. He never thought that Jay would actually succeed.

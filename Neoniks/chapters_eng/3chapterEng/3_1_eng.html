CHAPTER 3

THE OBNOXIOUS SISTER

Jay’s older sister, Phoebe, wanted to become a muse. That was the name of the magic beauties who inspired poets and artists to new works of art. They lived in the middle of the sea in Foam Castle and wore long silken gowns and hooded cloaks. Just meeting a muse who was flying around was considered good luck.

Phoebe tried to dress like them, decorated herself with flowers, and waved her hair into beautiful locks every morning.

After school Phoebe attended the Little Muse Club, and that day their coach showed the girls how to inspire students to do their homework.

When she came home, Phoebe wanted to have dinner, but she could not find her favorite bowl.

“Jay!” she yelled. “Did you take my bowl again?”

But her little brother did not reply.

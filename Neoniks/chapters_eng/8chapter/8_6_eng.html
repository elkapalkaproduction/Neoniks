“Please don't get distracted!” begged Jay. “You've got to save Mystie.”

Suddenly the cats cocked their ears, looked at each other, and fled every which way.

Phoebe heard a dull hum, little winged knights were heading straight for her. They were wearing armor and held sharp spears in their hands.

“Leave our flowerz-z-z alone!” buzzed the little knights.

Phoebe wanted to say that she hadn't touched anything, but at that very moment a tiny spear struck her seat.

“What are you doing?” yelled Phoebe and hurriedly pulled the Sky Scooter down.

Trying to get away, she flew into someone's hanging garden where shimmering pumpkins and squashes were dangling from a stone ceiling. Their leaves intertwined, and Phoebe barely avoided crashing into something.

Jay's worried voice came over the intercom, “What happened?”

“A-a-ah!” screamed Phoebe. “I've been attacked!”

“By who?”

But she did not have time to explain.

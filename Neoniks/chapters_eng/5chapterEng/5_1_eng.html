CHAPTER 5

SEA DOG


Sea Dog lived at the top of Cake Island in a building that looked like a lighthouse.

Phoebe and Jay heard his dreary howl a long way off.

“I don’t think your teacher is well at all,” said Phoebe. “Maybe we should call the doctor?”

They knocked, but no one answered.

“Looks like the door’s open,” said Jay, pushing at it.

The howl was cut off and they saw Sea Dog sitting on the steps of the spiral stairs, his paws tied to the railing behind his back.

The kids rushed to him and set him free.

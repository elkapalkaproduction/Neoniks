“What happened here?” asked Jay.

Sea Dog looked away in embarrassment.

“Yesterday the pirates attacked me and I fought them valiantly. But unfortunately, they won, so I couldn’t make it to school.”

When Sea Dog told his stories, he often got carried away and began making things up. Knowing this habit of his, he always wore his Conscience badge, which did not let him spin too long a yarn.

As soon as he mentioned pirates, the badge on his chest turned red and began flashing lights that looked like the ones on police cars.

“Fine, fine,” growled Sea Dog. “I’ll admit it!”

He took a tiny bubble wand from his pocket, dipped it into his flask of magic, and blew a bubble.

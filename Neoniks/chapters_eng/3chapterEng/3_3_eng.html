Humming quietly, the can flew towards Jay to shed the special Light of Knowledge on him, but he caught it in his fist like a fly.

“Careful!” yelled Phoebe. “You’ll crush my Inspiration! Let it go now!”

“No way!” said Jay.

Wings sprouted behind the kids’ backs. Following Jay up to the ceiling, Phoebe began chasing him around.

They became so noisy that Dad came up to the room.

“What’s going on up here?” he asked.

The Fount of Inspiration shot out of Jay’s hand, and the Light of Knowledge spilled onto Dad’s head.

 		    

“We’re in trouble now,” whispered Phoebe.

But instead of getting mad, Dad smacked his forehead. “I’ve got to water my plants!” he said and went out on the balcony to take care of his medicinal grapes that grew clusters of pills instead of berries.

“It worked!” gasped Phoebe and hugged Jay joyously. “Dad doesn’t have any homework to do, but he was inspired to do chores!”

But Jay did not want to celebrate. He came down slowly, sat at his desk, and went back to his game.

“What’s wrong?” asked Phoebe, flying up to her brother.

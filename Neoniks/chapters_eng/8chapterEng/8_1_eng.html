CHAPTER 8

BOTTOMLESS ISLAND

Jay and Phoebe had toured Bottomless Island many times. It was a dormant volcano with a huge well in its throat. Guides said you could go down forever and ever, but admittedly, none of the tourists ever went very far down because there were plenty of interesting things right at the top.

On the interior slopes of Bottomless Island there were caves and terraces that housed the most incredible animals and plants.

In addition, there were several underground villages, whose residents grew magic vegetable gardens and orchards.

Inside, Bottomless Island was filled with a special greenish air that was heavier and thicker than regular air. Those who spent any length of time there got used to applying more effort to doing everything. When they came out they moved at a higher speed and spoke in high cartoonish voices for a while.

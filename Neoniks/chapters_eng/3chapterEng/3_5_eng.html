Phoebe was amazed to learn that her little brother managed to make a magic creature.

“Who said this Justacreep could take other people’s foxes?” she fumed.

They could hear Dad’s song coming from the balcony:

Oh, my sweet purple vine,
Be filled with pills, so they may shine!
Wee pearls in the sun
Grow they ripe one by one.
     
“If I hadn’t caught your winged can, would it have inspired me to search for Mystie?” asked Jay.

Phoebe shook her head.

“We haven’t had Search Inspirations yet. But you can still fly to school tomorrow and ask where to find Mr. Justacreep. Do you want me to go with you?”

“Yes,” Jay sniffed and smiled. “Thanks a lot!”

It was nice to have an older sister, even if she did chase you around sometimes.

Beppo cast the spell, and a bright light flared behind Jay's back, his new wings flickering with tiny golden sparks.

“Hurray!” cried Jay and Phoebe and fell silent seeing the scary muzzle of the crocorhine above the edge of the well. It growled and began to throw clods of earth down at them.

Jay grabbed his sister under her arms. “Hold the map! ” he yelled. “We've got to fly away or the crocorhine will bury us!”

Heaving hard, Jay pulled his sister out of the well and they flew down the maze. The crocorhine followed them, stomping madly and howling. Hearing him, his relatives gathered up and soon the entire herd was chasing Jay and Phoebe.

“Hey, what's going on there?” Beppo's voice came out of the well. “Where did you find a crocorhine? If you need help, I can call the police!”

But there was no answer.

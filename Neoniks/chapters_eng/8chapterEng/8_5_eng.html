Phoebe goes to Bottomless Island
Rays of sun illuminated the ledges deep down, while colored flying carps with long fin-wings circled close to the surface and bit off the glimmering pieces of a floating giant dandelion.

“Phoebe, can you hear me?” Jay’s voice came over the intercom. “Are you all right?”

“Yep, everything’s fine,” she replied.

“Then start circling around and looking for Mr. Justacreep.”

“Okay, I’m going down now.”

When Phoebe flew into the greenish air, it felt as if a strong scented wind was blowing up at her.

“Are you there yet? What do you see?” asked Jay.

“Red flowers!” answered Phoebe, fascinated. They’re bigger than me and they are growing right out of the walls! Wow, winged cats! They’re flying around the flowers and licking off nectar!”

The knights kept throwing their spears at the Sky Scooter and by now it looked like a bur with little thorns.

“I see where you are now!” cried Jay. “I'll tell you where to go. Turn right… now duck… Slow down and go down!”

Jay moved Phoebe just like a video game character and, to her surprise, he really got her out of the garden maze.

The knights fell back and Phoebe could finally catch her breath. She found herself in somebody's backyard, where everything from the trees to the tool shed was upside down, on the underside of a huge flat rock jutting from the volcano wall.

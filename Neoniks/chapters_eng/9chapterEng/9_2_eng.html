The crocorhines had become used to the treat and, seeing Justacreep’s top hat, ran up to meet their friend.

“Ho, you brutes, how are you doing?” he asked as he fed them lacegrass leaves. The crocorhines slurped and grunted with pleasure.

After giving out treats, Justacreep slunk to the cave entrance. It was completely dark and Furcoat ordered Wanda to get the magic light going.

“I don’t wanna,” she whined. “I look so stupid in the magic light.”

“Are we supposed to wander around in the dark just to please you?” grumbled Justacreep.

As he shoved a ball of magic in her mouth, a bright glow flashed from inside Wanda’s head, in which not only her skull but also her brains could be seen.

“Bear with it, my beauty,” Justacreep smirked. “You’re suffering for a great cause.”

Finally, they reached a cave with the entrance blocked by strong oak gates. Justacreep unlocked them and walked in. Inside was a small submarine completely covered in demonite. It had a small rotor at its stern and a cannon that shot magic rays in the bows.

Justacreep turned on the light bulb on the ceiling, and Wanda could finally swallow the ball of magic and go back to her usual state. She jumped out of Justacreep’s hands and vigorously hopped around the room.

“Let’s hurry up and finish our submarine!” she cried.

Justacreep took off Furcoat, hung him up on a horned coatrack, and took the demonite out of his pocket.

“So much work! So much trouble!” he said holding the demonite against the light. “We’ve been collecting these rocks for over a year now. But the greatest actor of our times will sharpen claws in a locker room no more!”

“I just want it to work!” said Wanda impatiently.

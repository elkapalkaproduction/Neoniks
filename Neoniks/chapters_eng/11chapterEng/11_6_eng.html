They were nearing the greenhouse, when Wanda jumped into the bushes and beckoned to the staff, “Come here, handsome!” she whispered tenderly. “Let me kiss you!”

Forgetting everything, he plunged after her. Wanda jumped up and kissed the young face on the lips.

All the magic of the staff flowed into Wanda, and after a long kiss the staff clattered to the ground. Both its faces froze into wooden masks.

Wanda patted her stomach in satisfaction. “I think I’m going burst with all this magic!” she said, and hopped after Justacreep and Lola.

Magic tree seedlings were planted in trays and buckets and raised there for several years until their trunks were large enough to house the painted people.

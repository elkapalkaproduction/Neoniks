“Unfortunately, our fox has fleas and I am afraid you might catch them,” he said. “It's my mother's pet and she asked me to take it to the vet.”

“Oh well,” the dragon sighed and rose. “Time to go play. We've got an important game today.”

The dragons went into a huddle, stomped their paws, and rattled their armor.

Muscles – a ton!

Claws made to stun!

Winning on the run,

We're afraid of none!

Mystie could call for help till she was hoarse – no one would have heard her in the din.

When the dragons ran out of the room, Justacreep said to Mystie, “If you try to run away again, I'll put demonite in the cup with you.”

The little fox shrank away in fear; she figured that Justacreep would do any dirty trick to get his way.

“Have the fire hockey players left?” Furcoat's voice sounded hollow.

All this time he and Wanda had been hidden in one of the metal lockers.

“Take us out!” yelled the magic wand. “It's stuffy in here and Furcoat smells of dog!”

Justacreep opened the door and took them out.

“Where's the demonite?” asked Furcoat.

Justacreep patted his chest. “Right here, in a bag in my breast pocket.”

“Then hurry up and put me on – I am starving without your heat! Be careful though. Unlike you, Wanda and I are magical creatures and we can't touch demonite.”

Justacreep pulled Furcoat on and shoved Wanda into the buttonhole. “I guess we'll be going to Bottomless Island now. We've got plenty of lacegrass and demonite. Are we taking the fox?”

“Sure,” said Furcoat. “It'll come in handy.”

Mystie was even more scared. She felt Justacreep picking up her cup, and whined quietly with fear.

“Hello,” said an unfamiliar voice with a dragon accent. “Where do you sharpen claws here?”

“And who are you?” asked Justacreep suspiciously.

“An athlete,” replied the dragon. “My name's Harold the Unbeatable.”

“You're too young to be ‘the Unbeatable’,” grumbled Justacreep and put the cup back on the table. “Oh, well, let's see your claws here.”

Mystie couldn't decide what to do. Should she yell? But what if Justacreep followed up on his threat?

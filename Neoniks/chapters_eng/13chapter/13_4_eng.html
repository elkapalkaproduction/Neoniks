“Don't touch me!” yelled Justacreep, grabbing the toilet brush and brandishing it like a sword.

Finally Furcoat chased Mystie into a corner. “I got you now, you little shrimp,” he growled and got ready to squash her with his fist.

Just then the gates crashed open and a herd of crocorhines barged into the cave. They ran into the submarine by the entrance and demonite flew off it all over the cave.

In terror, Mystie covered her head with her paws but fortunately none of the rocks hit her. Furcoat managed to avoid demonite by jumping behind a pile of stones; the crocorhines that were touched by it passed out, while the rest ran away howling.

“Some sleeping beauties!” said Jay as he flew into the cave.

Phoebe was sitting on his shoulders and holding the glowing map in front of her.

“Mystie must be here somewhere!” she cried pointing to the blinking dot. “We were right to bring the crocorhines here and make them gate-crash!”

“Watch out!” yelled Mystie.

Furcoat jumped on the kids, but Jay managed to dodge and pick up the fox off the floor as they flew up to the ceiling. With a deafening roar, Furcoat began hurling pieces of rock at them.

“Well, are you scared?” shrieked Wanda in a dreadful voice.

“No,” said the kids.

They had all known the story of the Greedy Miner since their kindergarten days. Moreover, many of them had met him: so there was a skeleton who wandered the streets and moaned. So what?

“I don't think Wanda's a real teacher,” whispered Beppo to Jay. “She doesn't know how to deal with kids.”

“Yeah, and Mr. Justacreep is pretty strange, too,” agreed Jay. “I wonder where he's gone to.”

He had decided to show Mystie during recess, but he was so bored with Wanda's “lesson” that he could not take it anymore.

“Beppo, I won a magic fox,” he said quietly.

“No way!”

Beppo also liked to play video games and he often competed with Jay on who could reach the last level first.

“Look!” said Jay and pulled Mystie out of his backpack.

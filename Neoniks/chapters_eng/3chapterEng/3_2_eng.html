Seething with rage, Phoebe looked into his room. Just as she thought! Jay poured water into the first bowl that came handy, added a ball of magic to it, and created a screen for a multi-player video game.

It was times like these that Phoebe wanted to send her brother to the Brickoven Desert or some other place even further and nastier – just so he’d know not to take other people’s things!

She had a brilliant idea – what if she inspired Jay to study the Crazy Cactuses that grew in the desert? They were very interesting plants with quite a unique sense of humor – they threw prickly balls at the passersby and roared with laughter if they scored a hit. If Jay became interested in them, he’d ask their parents to send him to the Brickoven Summer Camp.

Phoebe had a tiny ball of magic left after the club session and it was just enough to create a little winged watering can that the muses called the Fount of Inspiration.

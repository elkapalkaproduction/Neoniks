CHAPTER 7

FIRE HOCKEY

When Mystie came to, she felt as if she'd been run over by a toy train – her body ached, her head was spinning, and her ears rang.

She was lying under an upturned plastic soda cup left on the wooden table. Mystie carefully lifted the edge of the cup and looked out.

It seemed that she was in a large room with a lot of metal lockers. Justacreep was there too, right by the door; he was sharpening the claws of a green dragon in shining armor.

Justacreep treated him very respectfully, not at all like he had treated the kids at school.

“You can't imagine how I admire you!” he was saying as he tried to catch the dragon's eye. “You are so strong, so powerful – just like a bulldozer!”

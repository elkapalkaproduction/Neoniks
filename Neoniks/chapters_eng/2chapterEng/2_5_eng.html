Mr. Justacreep propped the wand against the wall.

“Well, Wanda, you wanted to drill children. Here they are – have fun. Furcoat and I have some business to take care of.”

When he left, Wanda the wand hopped around the classroom and barked out commands:

“If you don’t listen to me I’ll turn you into rotten tomatoes! No talking, no breathing, no blinking! Just sit there and be frightened!”

“Why should we be afraid?” asked a redheaded girl named Ginger Pistachio.

“I’ll tell you such scary stories that your hair will rise on end!”

I’m off the hook, thought Jay. The only thing he was afraid of was questions about his homework.

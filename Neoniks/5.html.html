“Mommy!” Phoebe yelled from her room. “Jay's fooling around with magic again and not letting me go to sleep!”

Mom came back to Jay's room with Phoebe at her heels. “What's going on here? We heard a pop.”

Jay hid the fox under his pillow. “I just swatted a mosquito,” he said.

“Where did the light come from?” asked Phoebe suspiciously. “I saw a light under your door.”

“It was a firefly mosquito,” answered Jay. “It had a light bulb in its tummy.”

This didn't especially surprise Mom and Phoebe because there were all sorts of magic creatures on Cake Island.

“Try to go to sleep now,” Mom said and kissed Jay goodnight.

When Mom and Phoebe left, Jay dove under the blanket again. He could not believe his eyes – he had a real talking magic fox!

“Let's play, but we have to be quiet!” he whispered. “We can't have my parents find out about you or I'll get in trouble.”

The fox nodded and began tickling Jay with her soft paws by running all over him. They played for a long time, trying not to laugh too loudly; then the fox wrapped herself around Jay's wrist like a bracelet and a wave of violet light went over her soft fur.

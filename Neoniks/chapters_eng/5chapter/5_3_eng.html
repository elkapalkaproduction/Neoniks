The big Sea Dog sighed heavily and popped the bubbles.

“I didn't realize that the fur coat was alive,” said the old teacher. “And it, or rather he, is probably the one who is in charge of Justacreep. The fur coat told him to search my pockets and take away the key to the school museum.”

“What did they want it for?” asked Phoebe, astonished.

Sea Dog shrugged. “I have no idea. But that's not all! After they tied me to the railing, Justacreep called the school and told them in my voice that I was sick and that there will be a substitute. That man is a first-class actor!”

Three years ago his parents cast the Winged Spell on him, and Jay learned to sprout wings on his back whenever he needed them, just like the other Neonik kids.Naturally, these wings were very simple, but Jay hoped that one day he'd be able to create truly magnificent wings like his dad's. Dad was a senior magical consultant at the Unnatural History Museum, and his wings sparkled with neon lights.

After practicing magic a lot Jay managed to get a couple of curls on his wings.

He was very proud of this because his sister Phoebe, who was two years older, only had two dark spots on the tips of hers.

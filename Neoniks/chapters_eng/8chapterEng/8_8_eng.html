Phoebe is trying to escape from the flying soldiers
Jay moved Phoebe just like a video game character and, to her surprise, he really got her out of the garden maze.

The soldiers fell back and Phoebe could finally catch her breath. She found herself in somebody’s backyard, where everything from the trees to the tool shed was upside down, on the underside of a huge flat rock jutting from the volcano wall.

“Where do I go now?” asked Phoebe.

“Forward!” replied Jay.

Phoebe pulled the handlebars, Sky Scooter jerked, and she hit a wet sheet that was hanging out on the clothesline.

Phoebe fell off the Sky Scooter, and the last thing that Jay heard was her yell of “Mo-o-mmy!” that was getting fainter and fainter.

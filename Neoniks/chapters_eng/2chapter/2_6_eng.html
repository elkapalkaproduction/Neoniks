Wanda bounded to Jay's desk and banged her handle on the floor impatiently.

“Give that beast here this minute or I'll turn you into a dry booger!”

Jay went red. “I can't give her to you. She won't want to go.”

“Give it here!” bellowed Wanda.

“There is no need to yell,” said Mr. Justacreep, entering the classroom. He came up to Jay and asked gently, “May I meet your fox?”

“Mystie, show yourself, please,” whispered Jay.

The fox pushed her face out and wanted to sneak right back, but Mr. Justacreep caught hold of her tail and yanked her out.

“An excellent specimen!” he said and tucked Mystie into the pocket of his fur coat.

“Give her back!” cried Jay.

But Mr. Justacreep ignored him and simply said, “Class dismissed.”

When he left the classroom, Jay's friends rushed to his side and tried to comfort him.

“The teachers have no right to take our things!” Beppo raged. “It's robbery!”

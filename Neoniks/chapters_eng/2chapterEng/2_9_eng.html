Mr. Justacreep takes Mystie away


      Mr. Justacreep tucked Mystie into the pocket of his fur coat and, picking up Wanda, headed for the door.

“Give my fox back!” cried Jay.

But Mr. Justacreep ignored him and simply said, “Class dismissed.”

When he left the classroom, Jay’s friends rushed to his side and tried to comfort him.

“The teachers have no right to take our things!” Beppo raged. “It’s robbery!”

“Don’t worry, he’ll give the fox back,” said Ginger Pistachio. As long as she was happy her hair was red, but now it turned green with rage.

The door opened again and Jay jumped up, thinking that Mr. Justpuss was coming back, but it was the school principle.

“Turn your wands in and fly to the auditorium,” he ordered.

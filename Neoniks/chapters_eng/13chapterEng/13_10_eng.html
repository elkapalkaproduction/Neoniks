Everyone came to watch the parade – Cary, Sea Dog, and the entire fire hockey team. Even Ruben and Lola were there, although just like Jay and Phoebe, they tried to be quiet and not applaud too loudly. After spending a while inside Bottomless Island their voices were squeaky and they all moved at high-speed.

Then they watched the fireworks, which were splendid. After the Neoniks found out that the magic flower had been destroyed, everyone chipped in to replace the magic that had been lost.

When the tired but happy crowd decided to go home, Zoe the Turtle crawled into the square.

“Ha, you back already?” she said seeing Phoebe and Jay. “I’ve been walking all day to get here and find help!”

Jay and Phoebe had to smother their laughter as they thanked her for her efforts.

“By the way, what are your parents going to say about keeping that magic fox?” Zoe asked.

“Oh, we’ll deal with our parents,” replied Jay. “What Rusty Pomodoro will  have to say to this is a totally different matter. In any case, we’re going to have an unforgettable summer break!”

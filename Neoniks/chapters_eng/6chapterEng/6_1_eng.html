CHAPTER 6

HAROLD THE DRAGON

After Sea Dog went home, Phoebe and Jay stayed at the school entrance for a long time trying to think of what to do next.

“We have only one clue,” said Phoebe. “If Mr. Justacreep is such a fine actor, other actors might know about him too.”

“Or billposters,” said Jay and pointed to a young dragon who was posting a new playbill on the wall of a building across the street.

The playbill announced that, in honor of the Jubilant Moon Festival, Oversea Money Dwarves would perform popular melodies by jingling gold coins hidden in their purses.

The wind was pretty strong and the dragon had trouble spreading out the poster – it kept getting away from him and rolling over his head.
